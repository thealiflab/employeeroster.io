Features

Everything your team needs, nothing they don't

A complete rostering system built for WordPress — no third-party accounts, no monthly per-seat costs.

📅

Shift Scheduling

Create shifts with direct assignment or open slots. Employees self-assign from open shifts up to the slot limit. Weekly roster and monthly calendar views. Copy an existing week to rebuild schedules instantly.

⏱

Clock In / Clock Out

Staff clock in from the WP dashboard or the frontend portal. Break deductions and shift linking included. One active session per user enforced — managers can edit or correct any record.

🏖

Leave Management

Annual and sick leave requests with a manager approval workflow. Optional balance tracking auto-accrues entitlements per job type through the year, with manual adjustment support.

📊

Timesheets & Payroll

Weekly summaries with hours worked, pay rate calculations, optional tax, and a configurable daily labour budget. Weekend penalty rate multipliers apply automatically to Saturday and Sunday shifts.

🔄

Shift Swap Requests

Employees request swaps with an optional target employee and reason. Managers review and approve — shift assignments update automatically on approval.

📆

Staff Availability

Each employee sets weekly recurring availability with day-level time ranges. Availability hints appear inside the shift assignment modal — Available, Unavailable, or Not Set — so managers avoid conflicts without leaving the screen.

✉️

Email Notifications

Automated plain-text emails via wp_mail() for new accounts, shift assignments, timing changes, and leave decisions. Triggered server-side — no employee login required.

📱

Frontend Employee Portal

Five shortcode pages — Dashboard, My Shifts, Leave, Clock In/Out, and Availability — let staff manage everything without ever touching WP Admin.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Separate Manager, Supervisor, and Employee roles with granular capabilities. Profile Mode blocks employee and supervisor WordPress logins entirely while keeping email notifications active.

🌐

Public Roster

Embed a read-only roster on any public page with the [emp_roster_public] shortcode — no login required. Ideal for displaying schedules to customers or walk-in staff.

📤

Exports & Reports

Export timesheet data to CSV for payroll processing. Generate a colour-coded, print-ready PDF of the current roster directly from the manager toolbar.

⏳

Weekly Hour Limits

Configure weekly hour thresholds per job type — Full-time 38h, Part-time 24h, Casual unlimited. Employees approaching or exceeding their limit are flagged in the roster view.

Setup

Up and running in minutes

1

Install & Activate

Upload to /wp-content/plugins/ and activate. 7 database tables and 3 custom roles are created automatically.

2

Configure

Add your departments, locations, and pay settings from Employee Roster > Settings.

3

Add Staff

Create staff accounts with job type, pay rate, and role. Welcome emails with login links are sent automatically when email notifications are enabled.

4

Build the Roster

Create shifts, assign employees, and publish. Staff see their schedules immediately — in WP Admin or the frontend portal.
